Ag&F Data custodian form

The way in which CSIRO manages its data assets is changing. In preparation for this, within Ag&F we are looking to learn more about Ag&F data assets and how they are managed. To begin this process we are requesting that all Ag&F project leaders to nominate a data custodian for all active projects. A data custodian is someone who manages your project data. The data custodian for your project could be yourself or someone from your project. Thank you for your time.
